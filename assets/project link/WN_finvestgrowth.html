Finvest Growth is a wealth advisory service targeting HNIs and new investors. I designed a conversion-focused landing page that clarifies their value proposition, builds trust through advisor credibility, and simplifies lead capture. The layout reduces cognitive load, highlights key offerings, and improves sign-up flow for marketing campaigns.

View Prototype

Overview

The client needed a landing page specifically for performance marketing. Their previous communication was text-heavy and lacked a clear narrative for convincing new users. I developed a clean visual hierarchy, trust-reinforcing elements (credentials, testimonials, team), and a simplified inquiry form.

The result is a persuasive, modern design optimized for engagement and lead conversions.

Process

  1. 1. Research & Insight Gathering
    • Reviewed brand messaging and existing website flow
    • Analyzed common financial landing page patterns
    • Identified trust factors essential for HNI audiences
    • Defined sections using Awareness → Trust → Action
  2. 2. UX Structure & Content Strategy
    • Top-to-bottom persuasion flow
    • Value → Trust → Advisors → Offer → CTA
    • Lean form fields to reduce drop-off
  3. 3. Wireframes
    • Low-fi layout to validate information order
    • Scannable sections across breakpoints
    • Space for testimonials, advisors, philosophy
  4. 4. Visual Design
    • Clean white + lavender theme for clarity
    • Graphic modules for investment approach
    • Friendly imagery to reduce decision anxiety
  5. 5. Final Prototype & Handoff
    • Responsive screens delivered in Figma
    • Component styles & spacing documented

Outcome

  • Clearer communication around company credibility and investment benefits
  • Stronger user trust with advisor profiles and testimonials
  • Reduced form complexity to improve conversion likelihood
  • Ready-to-launch marketing funnel asset supporting paid ad campaigns
