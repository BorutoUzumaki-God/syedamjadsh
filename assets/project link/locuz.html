View Prototype

Overview

Locuz offering a wide set of enterprise solutions. Their website content was strong, but the service pages lacked visual hierarchy, consistency, and modern UI standards—making it difficult for users to understand offerings quickly.

This redesign focused on organizing complex service information into clear, structured layouts, establishing a reusable component system, and aligning the visual identity with a more contemporary enterprise look and feel.

The Challenge

  • The website had inconsistent layouts and lacked visual hierarchy.
  • Each service page contained large blocks of text, making it hard to scan or understand quickly.
  • There was no unified structure to showcase offerings, benefits, and differentiators.
  • Graphic elements were outdated and did not reflect Locuz's technical expertise.

Design Goals

  • Improve Clarity: Break down service information into easy-to-read visual sections.
  • Establish Consistency: Create reusable page templates & component styles.
  • Enhance Brand Trust: Use modern visual language inspired by cybersecurity and enterprise UI patterns.
  • Simplify Handoff: Deliver clean Figma structure for seamless developer implementation.

Process & What I Did

  1. Reviewed existing pages & collected content

    Studied previously published pages, marketing assets, and sales materials to understand positioning and user intent.

  2. Defined structure & page flow

    Created a unified page template used across all service pages:

    • Hero Banner
    • Problem Context
    • Core Value Proposition
    • Service Offerings
    • Benefits
    • Why Choose Locuz
    • Partnerships & CTA
  3. Low-fidelity wireframes

    Mapped text-heavy content into visual sections for faster scanning and clearer hierarchy.

  4. Visual design & component system
    • New illustration style, icons, and section patterns
    • Consistent spacing, color, and typography rules
  5. Figma handoff to developers

    Organized components, page specs, and annotations for a smooth build and future page expansion.

Outcome

The redesigned Enterprise Solutions pages improved readability, trust, and navigation clarity for business decision-makers exploring security and digital transformation services. The structured design system is now reusable, helping Locuz expand additional service pages efficiently.
