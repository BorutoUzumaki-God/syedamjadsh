A conversational AI app that provides clear, trustworthy wellness guidance for everyday health concerns.

MediAssist is an AI-driven health chat companion designed to help users understand symptoms, receive supportive guidance, and make informed wellness decisions. The focus was to create a warm, human, and reassuring chat experience that provides clarity without overwhelming medical jargon.

View Prototype

Overview

Users often struggle with online medical information — it’s confusing, contradictory, and sometimes frightening. MediAssist helps users handle everyday health situations (like fever, fatigue, minor injuries) at home with safe, conversational, and evidence-based guidance.

The goal was to make the experience empathic, calming, and easy to follow for users aged 18–60+.

Problem Summary

Users found existing AI health chats:

  • Too robotic and emotionally cold
  • Overloaded with dense text
  • Lacking clear next steps or support
  • Hard to use for older adults or users with lower digital familiarity

We needed to redesign the chat so it felt:

  • Human, friendly, and supportive
  • Guided, not overwhelming
  • Lacking clear next steps or support
  • Visually light and easy to read

Research & Insights


1. Methods used

8 user interviews (18–60 yrs), 3 remote task tests, chat-log review (200+ turns), quick competitor scan (Ada, K Health, WebMD).

2. Entry anxiety

Users feared “misdiagnosis” from AI; they wanted to know who made the advice and when to see a doctor.

3. Trust cues matter

Clear credentials, sources, and last-updated dates increased confidence and completion.

4. Language & tone

Medical phrasing felt cold; plain, reassuring language reduced drop-offs in the first 3 messages.

5. Accessibility

Larger tap targets and 16–18px base type helped older users; high-contrast success/error states aided clarity.

6. Safety boundaries

Users sometimes asked about serious symptoms; automatic handoff to human/doctor with disclaimers was expected.

7. History & recall

Gentle reminders (e.g., “re-check fever after 6 hrs”) were preferred over generic push spam.

Design Approach

1. Paper Sketch → Low Fidelity Wireframe → High Fidelity UI → Final Design

You followed a progressive shaping process to validate clarity and emotional tone early.

2. Conversational UX Writing
  • Shifted from instructional tone → compassionate guidance
  • Introduced phrases like “You’re in a judgment-free space. I’m here to help.”
3. UI Enhancements
  • Larger chat bubbles
  • Clear visual separation of tips, analysis, suggestions
  • Soft, calming color palette

The Solution

The final design feels empathetic, human-centered, and approachable, guiding users step-by-step through their health queries with confidence and reassurance.

Result

  • Reduced cognitive overload
  • Increased user trust in the AI responses
  • Improved task completion (users no longer get “stuck” in conversation flows)
  • More emotionally supportive wellness experience
